This is information and notes for Eric's Campaign!

It is 1391 in the year of the Lord Zeus

The local village, Hose (h-oe-z-uh), has survived in the mediocre lands for several generations, despite the periodic raiding party, largely because of the success in keeping most of the villagers as capable as possible!

Early on, the villagers of Hose decided to keep everyone active and trained in some adventuring capability rather than a militia!
This was because they, like many other villages, were on the outskirts of Tropolis, a large city near the meeting point of several rivers, and were therefore tasked by the local leadership with keeping the riffraff from invading.

Suddenly, on a day no different than any other, the entirety of the village felt a tremendous shaking of the earth, unlike anything anyone had ever felt!
Many of the more foolish villagers cried for the earth to swallow them whole to end it quickly since obviously the entire world was being rent apart.
After everything had settled down, the leader of the village, Uzul (a monstrous, grotesque, kind and slightly cowardly Half-Orc), sent a team out to investigate for damage to the village and surrounding lands as well as see if they could identify the source.
He instructed them to start with Moonshadow Field, the best grazing field from nearby, before moving on.

They returned quickly as the field had been completely Replaced by a massive castle larger than even the closest mountains, which were big enough to house several Different dwarven clans!!!

Uzul, not wanting to go himself, decided to hold a vote!
The people of Hose would decide to either have 6 volunteers to go explore the castle, or everyone from the village would be entered into a raffle, and 6 names would be drawn to go explore.
The villagers turned out more cowardly than expected and it was almost entirely unanimous for the raffle, hoping to not be chosen.

You Six were the selected few and will be off to explore soon!!!
