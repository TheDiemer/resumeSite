This is the most recent recap of the adventures of The Revengers of Mesegea of Eturn Prime, a.k.a. Eric's Campaign!

This is the recap for our most recent session, which took place on August 3rd, 2022:

Heading straight into the home after little conversation with Flint, the Revengers quickly heard the crashing noise the harengon referred to.
Briefly exploring the tiny home, Duvu lead the party into the only room (confirmed to be the bedroom) as the only location the noise could be coming from.
Using his well trained ears and delicate fingers, Duvu found a hole underneath the bookshelf. After attempting, and failing, to quietly move the bookshelf with Adelvir, Duvu discretely called for the stronger team members to come help.
Offending Wrench by softly suggesting the bot is incapable of being quiet, Duvu was quickly proven right as Wrench rushed over to it, had to adjust his grip a few times, and then loudly dragged it away from the wall. The party took in several details as they stared down into the gaping hole in the floor:

  1. There is no light down the stairs
  2. The hole itself looks like it was dug from the home side, along with the first several, roughly hewn stone steps
  3. About 30 ft into the darkness, the stairs transition into nicely formed, well planned and carved steps that look older than the dig near the top

Opting to try and regain any amount of secrecy, the party formed a chain with Wrench situated in the middle, as the only member without the ability to see in the dark.
They decended in the darkness for close to a minute, with the loud crashing noise growing louder and louder, though still avoiding any sense of rhythm or pattern.
As they reached the landing platform next to a door that clearly separated them from the source of the noise, most of the party heard a brief, dull splat.

Turning around in the darkness, the majority of the party was able to see that their sight was...impared?
No...there was a large cube of some sort of gooey material that you all could see through but it was easy to identify.
Moving swiftly, Adelvir and Ayayron slammed it with magic before Gisli rushed in and demolished the goo with two Viscious swings of her hammer. As they regain their senses in the darkness, the crashing noise continues...
Leaving nothing left to do, the party entered the room and found 9 undead soldiers, with bows raised and swords slung, and 1 large, rotting undead minotaur slamming the back of his axe into the ground.
Jumping straight into action, the party did their best to whiddle away at the soldiers, leaving Gisli (and a guest appearance by Wrench) to put a beat down on the minotaur.
As the soldier's numbers dwindled, Ayayron, who spent the majority of the fight using what was clearly darker magics, brings one of the remaining soldiers down to a pile of bones using another dark spell...but stays connected for a moment as it then...reassembles itself and stares as him...expectantly...

Will the Sapphireshapers be able to pay up? and When? What about Hephaestus?

What is going on with Ayayron? Does he now have a zombie under his control?!?!
