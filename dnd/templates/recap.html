This is the most recent recap of the adventures of The Revengers of Mesegea of Eturn Prime, a.k.a. Eric's Campaign!

This is the recap for our most recent session, which took place on June 29th, 2022:

Continuing to track our split party, we find Ayayron resuming his exploration of the 3rd floor as a gaseous cloud!
Before moving down to the 2nd floor, he finished exploring the master bedroom and found a closet full of nice clothes and a Very sizable bathroom, including a large, perfectly circular bathtub.
On this new floor he found more sleeping dwarves, an office with a Small note in a drawer, a guard that he easily dodged, and a Linen closet Full of nice linens of all kinds as well as what appears to be the entrance to a secret tunnel or panel at the back of the room...

The Distraction Division was doing a particularly good job of sneaking as they explored the secret basement finding 4 doors:

  • a Huge high quality steel door with a sizable lock, blocking what appears to be a safe
  • a set of double doors that led to what appears to be a boardroom
  • a Master workshop with the nicest set of diamond smithing tools and a teleportation circle
  • a large workshop with 7 workstations with diamond smithing tools, a drafting table with blank blueprints, a large forge, a diamond pickaxe, and a ladder leading up into the home.

Failing to break into the safe and taking a set of diamond smithing tools + the pickaxe with them, the Disctraction Division headed up the ladder into the unknown. The Sneak Squad did just that and made their way across the 1st floor, finding their way into a large library with floor to ceiling bookshelves on 3 walls, a billiards table, 6 fluffy chairs, and a large stairwell leading up to the second floor.
Finding a few books of interest, Krasus grabbed three scruffy looking, well-loved and used books from the shelves:
  • Refining with Mesegea
    • Extremely well-worn pages discuss the Sea of Tropolis.
  • Grinders, Polishers, Drill Bits & More!
    • Largely about the various tools used in refining gems.
  • The Big ol' Book o' Rocks
    • Largely about the various methods for extracting gems from Eturn.

Putting those books away, Duvu and Adelvir both heard the footsteps of a large half-orc woman starting to descend the steps nearby.
Throwing a plan together by himself, Krasus told Duvu to hide and grabbed Adelvir, yanking him into a space kind of behind the stairs, he Yelled to draw her to them faster.
After she came down the rest of the way, they tied her up after she called for help both in the house and on a sending stone.
They were able to handle her Just as another guard descended the same stairwell, only to be handled with magic from both Krasus and Adelvir!
The Sneak Squad finally headed upstairs to the 2nd floor primarily taking the time to explore the hallway with its desks, and stepping out onto the Patios!

As the guard yelled for help, Wrench and Gisli had found themselves climbing up into the Linen closet with Ayayron!
The newly expanded Distraction Division decided descending back down the ladder to give Ayayron a chance to open the safe.
Unfortunately, between his thieves' tools and creating a stone door in the wall, Ayayron was Also unable to get into the safe!

Will The Sneak Squad reunite with their second half?
Will they find anything that Ayayron didn't?
Will The Revengers find a way into the safe?
