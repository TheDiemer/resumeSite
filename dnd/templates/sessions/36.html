Session Thirty Six

Between their weapons, their magic, the assassins, and a T-REX, the Revengers quickly tore through the remaining necromancers and zombies till nothing was left above ground.
Taking a short rest to catch their breath and to searches the bodies, the party setup safe ground on the second floor for a bit.
Heading down into the basement after their nap, the Revengers found more necromancers and their undead!
After many stabs in the dark and damaging AoE spells, everyone against the Revengers laid dead on the ground, not moving anymore.
