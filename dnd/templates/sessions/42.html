Session Fourty Two

Stepping out of the Palace and into a fresh Monday Morning, and led by the nearly cantankerous Gisli, The Revengers reviewed their pending tasks and queries:

  • The Sapphireshapers are working on collecting payment still.
  • The King and his Royal Court are aware of Udgred and are building a Platoon to help.
  • Several local jobs left pending as Iris Messages.
  • A Job from Rizzo that will take ~2 weeks.
  • Persephone predicted her Mother plans to do something with Udgred on the coming Solstice in 11 days.

And opted to continue to develop their local reputation, choosing to work jobs around Tropolis as a way to wait out some time.
 Thinking a fair amount of Drinking will come with it, the party chooses to help out the local Official Tavern of Dionysus, The Manic Panther.
Finding a loud party happening in the middle of the morning, The Revengers find and meet with their Patron, a very pretty elf by the name of Liluth Omarel.
It turns out Liluth is taking some initiative, hoping to gain positive attention from the Owner so she can be promoted to Manager over the current idiot, Olo Goodchild, a halfling she describes as utterly useless.
She is hiring you as bodyguards for a night or two, specifically to keep out and scare off a small gang of Dionysus followers who believe the bar is misrepresenting Dionysus...but choosing to express that through drunken acts of violence IN The Manic Panther.
 The group is primarily a group of adolescent Kobolds who can be identified by hissing, their symbol (which they are known to blast on walls as graffiti using color spray), and saying their group name: The Serpent Riders!
Oh, and they end up pretty angry once they are in the tavern, so angry people might be a clue too!

Setting Wrench up at the back of the bar, the rest of the party took up shop out front of the bar and began to bounce!
Quickly establishing a required phrase "Sally sells sea shells down by the sea shore" and found a few kobold trying to sneak in!
Adelvir managed to trip them up and scared the leader of this grouping and then went right back to testing people with their phrase for several hours!
Having already let the rest of those kobold's friends inside, Krasus chased them down, asked them some more probing questions, and warned Wrench to keep an eye on them.
Gisli also found her way inside the tavern for a time, as she shared battle stories with a dwarf that came through the line.
