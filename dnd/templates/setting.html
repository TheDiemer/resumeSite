This is information and notes for Eric's Campaign!

  • Hose
    • A small village of a hundred or so past, present, or future adventurers.
    • On the outskirts of Tropolis!
  • Tropolis
    • A large city with satellite villages surrounding it
    • Centered near the River Chalcedon and the nearby Mountain Range near where these meet the Sea
  • Moonshadow Field
    • Common grazing field for Hose
  • Catterborough Keep
    • Mysterious Castle that Appeared in the middle of Moonshadow Field
  • Oractus Bluff
    • Large mountains on the otherside (NorthEast) of Moonshadow Fields from Hose
    • Separates the Greater Tropolis Area from the Halcyone Sea
    • Known to house several Dwarven Clans
  • Elytos
    • One of the four Inner Ring Towns
    • Home of Tropolis University
    • Home of the ΗΟΡΣΕ Fraternity!
  • Philonia
    • One of the eight surrounding villages (like Hose)
    • Known as the farming srrounding village.
    • Known for its annual "Running Of The Cows" Festival.
