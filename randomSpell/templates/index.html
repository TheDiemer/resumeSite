Sufit Roerc-Tisi

Base Details

  • Tisi is a Taracot Male.
  • Tisi is a Sufit (Hand, a.k.a. Lowest Tier or builders) of the Roerc family.
  • Tisi has recently discovered that he has magical powers!
  • Tisi was banished, forever, but believes he was simply sent on a quest to earn repentence.
  • Tisi is not very bright.
